LETTER FROM THE STUDENT DIRECTOR

Dear Participants,

It is with great pleasure that I, Student Director of The Joust, on behalf of the entire organising committee, welcome you to the fifth edition of The Joust, to be held on 28th and 29th of September, 2019.

Since its inception, The Joust has grown exponentially. With a plethora of events, a stellar judge panel and a dedicated organizing committee, the 5th edition is set to host 8 different literary and debating events expecting a participation of over 500 delegates across Northern India. It is much more than a two-day literary fest. It is a platform uniting people from over fifty different colleges to solve the most daunting global issues.

Placing quality over quantity in every event we conduct, not only do we take pride in being one of the most prestigious literary festivals of the circuit, but at the same time, we understand the responsibility that comes along with this position. With the focus lying on propagating the power of knowledge and providing an enriching experience at the same time, we at The Joust strive to inspire all participants to develop the ability to analyse complex situations, understand the multi-faceted nature of problems and grow tolerant enough to actively listen to friends and foes alike.

With a dedicated Organising Team, an alluring campus, and a diverse range of competitions, your presence is all we seek. On behalf of the entire organizing committee, I invite you to join us at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology for The Joust 2019.

Salil Shukla
Student Director

About JIIT


Jaypee Institute of Information Technology is an institute of higher education deemed to be university in Sector 62, Noida. It has 7 academic departments and it offers programs in technical education at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctorate level.

The college is known for its unmatched academic curricula and a plethora of extracurricular activities which provide all round holistic development of each and every student. With several hubs managed by the students themselves which focus on nurturing various skills and talents along with inculcating knowledge in fields as diverse as dance, music, theatre, literature, robotics, game development, multimedia, coding, fitness and the arts, JIIT has a lot to offer. There are also several student run Non-Governmental Organisations in this college that have pledged their contribution to environmental and social causes.

The college campus boasts of world class infrastructure, high tech laboratories and equipment, a modern Learning Resource Centre, and other amenities. The campus has a vibrant ambience with ample opportunities for students and mentors to grow and thrive alike.

About The Joust


The Joust is the Literary Fest of JIIT which has earned a name for itself with every succeeding edition making a mark in the entire Delhi-NCR community. The fest features various forms of literary events ranging from poetry and creative writing to debating and journalism. As the name suggests, The Joust is a battle of words where participants battle their wits and voice their opinions out loud. It also helps students to connect with real-world issues affecting the society that they belong to.

TWith every succeeding edition of The Joust, the quality and participation has increased exponentially. The dynamic nature of this event makes it a great opportunity for all our partners to enhance the reach of their organization and for all participants to be a better version of themselves.

Events

National Integration Council

National Integration council empowers one to combat the evils of communalism, casteism, regionalism, linguism, and narrow-mindedness hoping to create a more tolerant and peaceful society.

International Organization for Migration

International Organization for Migration tackles the issues faced by migrants and helps in framing cost-effective solutions for the same.

UNESCO

UNESCO facilitates one to build peace in the minds of men and women in order to ensure equality of opportunity in all aspects and to achieve sustainable development.

Tarksangram

Tarksangram is a two-day multi-format Hindi debate that witnesses heated arguments encouraging students to indulge in critical thinking.

Cerebrate

Cerebrate is an English Conventional Debate which provides a platform to express conflicting views and give a voice to opinions through discussion and deliberation.

The Press

'The Press' allows participants to act as unbiased journalists and photographers reporting on the proceedings of each simulation in the Conference and capturing every emotion along the way.

Inquizitive

Inquizitive is a General Knowledge Quiz for people who have a thirst for knowledge that can only be quenched with the right answers.

Penned

Penned is a creative writing competition which helps one to showcase the power of their thoughts and imagination when put into words

Verses

Verses is a bilingual slam poetry event where heartfelt verses from different voices win and break hearts at the same time. ‌

Why Join Us?

LEGACY OF JOUST

The first edition of Joust was organised in 2015, with the aim to create a new platform for debaters, poets, writers and photographers. The Joust was meant to rekindle the spirit of Jaypee Youth Parliament, and expand its horizons to include other events apart from committee negotiations. The Joust 2017 went a step further, comprising of seven major events. The Literary Festival reached its peak in 2018 in its fourth edition, with 7 events, 400 participants and a stellar Executive Board. We aim to surpass all the previous editions with The Joust 2019.

QUALITY EVENTS

The Joust 2019 comprises of 9 highly engaging events. It will have 3 unique committees, a multi-format Hindi debate, a english conventional debate, a slam poetry competition, a creative fiction competition, a general knowledge quiz and a press event. All the events will be presided over by highly experienced Executive Board members and judges. With exciting prizes for the winners, the Joust has a motto of quality over quantity. This edition is going to set a benchmark of quality debate and discussion with a huge number of debaters, MUNers, poets, writers and photographers from across the country participating.

HOSPITALITY

The Joust has an extremely dedicated and hard working organising committee committed to make the Literary Festival an unparalleld experience for its participants. With over 40 members distributed across 6 major departments, the team has pledged to fully dedicate its time to make this event bigger than ever before, in terms of participation, quality, judges as well as an overall experience. The team has been highly regarded in the past for being helpful and hospitable to the delegates and the team hopes to continue that trend this year as well.
